Guidelines

Login / sign up
To access HealthPal's personalized features, users must create an account and log in securely. This ensures data privacy and enables us to deliver tailored health recommendations based on individual needs.
Start the session
Upon login, users can initiate a session by entering their health-related questions or concerns. HealthPal's AI-powered chatbot will analyze the input and provide relevant health insights.
Track your progress
HealthPal enables users to track their health progress over time. By recording health data and behaviors, users can monitor their improvements and make informed decisions.
Previous session
Users can access their previous chat sessions, providing continuity and reference for ongoing health discussions. This feature ensures a seamless and comprehensive health experience.
Rewards
As users actively engage with HealthPal and follow its recommendations, they earn rewards and incentives. These rewards act as motivating factors, encouraging positive health habits and long-term commitment to well-being.
